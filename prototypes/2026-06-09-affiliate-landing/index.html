FAQ

How and when do I get paid?

Commissions are paid out monthly through Tolt. You can choose PayPal or bank transfer. Once a referral's first payment clears the 30-day refund window, your commission is approved and paid in the next monthly cycle.

Is there a minimum payout?

Yes — you can withdraw once your balance reaches $50. Anything below that rolls over to the next cycle.

What's the commission?

You earn 50% of your friend's first purchase — one of the highest cash rates in the category.

Is the 50% only on the first purchase?

Yes — you earn 50% of your friend's first payment. Yearly plans mean a bigger first payment, so a bigger payout for you.

How long does the referral cookie last?

The referral cookie lasts 30 days, so you still get credit if your friend signs up within a month of clicking your link.

Who can join?

Anyone — creators, marketers, agencies and everyday Buzz fans. Signing up is free and takes minutes.

Can I refer myself?

No. Self-referrals don't qualify for commission. Referrals must be genuine new users.

Can I run paid ads?

You can promote your link across most channels, but bidding on Buzz brand terms in paid search isn't allowed. When in doubt, reach out before running campaigns.

Where do I track earnings?

Everything lives in your Tolt dashboard — clicks, conversions and payouts update in real time.
